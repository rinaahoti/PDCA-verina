# MSO Maestro 5 Demo Script

**Duration**: 3-5 Minutes
**Actor**: Product Manager / Sales Engineer

---

### Scene 1: Introduction & Login (0:00 - 0:45)
**Goal**: Show ease of access and secure entry.
1.  Open the App (`index.html`).
2.  **Say**: "Welcome to MSO Maestro 5. We're starting at the secure login gateway."
3.  **Action**: Click the **"Sophia (Owner)"** demo button.
4.  **Say**: "I'm logging in as Sophia, a Process Owner. Notice the instant access to her personalized workspace."

### Scene 2: The Cockpit (0:45 - 2:00)
**Goal**: Demonstrate the "Command Center" concept (Owner vs. Worker).
1.  **Action**: Land on **Cockpit**. Point to the top section "My ToDos".
2.  **Say**: "Here is the Cockpit. It intelligently separates concerns. At the top, 'My ToDos' are items waiting for *my* direct action."
3.  **Action**: Point to "My Topics" (bottom section).
4.  **Say**: "Below, 'My Topics' gives me oversight of everything I own, even if others are working on it. Notice the **Traffic Lights**—Green is on track, Red would be critical."

### Scene 3: Measure Workspace & Data Integrity (2:00 - 3:30)
**Goal**: Show the 3-pane anatomy and History Log.
1.  **Action**: Click on **"Outdated Process Model"** (or any measure) in the list.
2.  **Action**: Highlight the 3 zones:
    -   **Action Bar** (Top): "Commands like Save and Print."
    -   **Structure Pane** (Left): "The PDCA lifecycle steps (Plan-Do-Check-Act)."
    -   **Data Pane** (Center): "The relevant fields for the *current* step."
3.  **Action**: Type something in the "Description" field.
4.  **Action**: Click **Save** in the Action Bar.
5.  **Action**: Point to the **History Log** in the bottom left.
6.  **Say**: "Crucially, every change is audited. See here? It logged my update instantly with time and user."

### Scene 4: Initiating Work (3:30 - 4:30)
**Goal**: Show the "New" flow and Templates.
1.  **Action**: Click **"+ Neu"** (New) in the sidebar.
2.  **Say**: "Starting new work is standardized."
3.  **Action**: In the modal, select **"PDCA Measure"** template.
4.  **Action**: Enter Title: "Q3 Safety Audit". Pick a future date.
5.  **Action**: Click **"Start Workflow"**.
6.  **Say**: "The system instantly spins up a new workspace with the correct lifecycle steps and rules pre-configured."

### Scene 5: Conclusion (4:30 - 5:00)
1.  **Action**: Click **Logout** in top right.
2.  **Say**: "That is MSO Maestro 5—structured, auditable, and easy to use."
